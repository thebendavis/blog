About Me

I recently completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of California, Davis where I worked with Professor Hao Chen in the Computer Security Lab. My research interests include computer security, privacy, and mobile systems. I am passionate about building software that allows us to better understand and control the systems on which we depend.
